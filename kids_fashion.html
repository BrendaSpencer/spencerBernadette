kids fashion

When shopping for my kids, i always like to look for unique items. Sometimes it can be expensive, but most of the time that’s not the case. I usually mix them together. you’ll be suprised how well it works. I’ll be posting some of my ideas and mixes with name brands

Boyish sandals

These sandals I bought at vertbaudet, You can find pretty good sales there but you have to act fast since a lot of these promotions won’t last long.

lacoste sneakers

These boys sneakers I bought at usc.co.uk for a great price, you do need to consider the delivery fee of 8€ outside of the UK. and the cost to send it back if the size is not right.

Girly Sandals

These beautiful sandals I bought at vertbaudet. they have four more color choices wich are black, white, silvery rainbow en fushia, but I liked this the most and so does my daughter. It is real leather and it smells like it too. it is a little tight on the toes but i asume it’ll stretch out after wearing them a few times. The zipper on the back makes it easy to put on. She can be a little lazy some times so she’s very happy that the clasps can stay closed!

reused halloween

The black skirt was an item from the h&m halloween collection. but to wear it only once seems a bit wastefull so I searched for something to make it suitable for everyday wear. Then I stumbled onto the mustard colored sweater in zara. I think it works very well together!

Daily Basics

Nothing beats a basic jeans with a nice shirt and for everyday jeans, I like those of h&m very much. The shirt is from zara, with the reversive stars it was a big hit. A very happy girl☺

accessorize

when I was brainstorming what to do with my daughter her hair, I came across the butterfly and the flowers, wich are actually for decoration. After i curled her hair, I tried to put them in and they just looked adorable.

The gold plated hairclips I bought at amazon, they where a pack of 8 pieces it was a feather, moon, bow and triangle. all of them where in ‘gold’ and ‘silver’ color. and they are nice for a special occasion but also very cute for every day use. And not only for girls as I use them too.

Last but not least: the bag. Wich I find very adorable. I ordered it by vertbaudet, it is nicely made and my daughter loves it and that’s the most important.

Lacoste Girls

These amazing sneakers, I bought at usc.co.uk. You do need to take in account the delivery fee outside of the uk, wich is 8€ and to send items back, it also has to be paid by you. I never tried to send things back, so I do not know how it really works. I always make sure the sizes are right.

expencive vs cheaper

The white butterfly dress was a sale in h&m, the vest wich i bought at river woods. This is a classic example of something expensive and a little cheaper. I also bought this jeansvest at zara just for those times you want to toughen it up☺

Another nice match is this pretty pink dress from h&m and a very nice vest with a nice shimmer from river woods.

Little Gentleman

I bought this at verbaudet. A very adorable suit for my,then, 3 year old, but I do feel that vertbaudet can be a little overpriced for what they deliver. none the less it still is an amazing outfit that I kept because of the nice details like the bowtie en the poche☺

London Chichi dress

I have bought this beautiful dress for my girl since we have an important event coming up. I ordered it by zalando, wich I did not find too expensive for such a dress. The embroidered flowers are nicely done. And she looks stunning in it, the back zipper is nicely fitted and the little opening opward towards the button is super cute☺ the skirt might be a tad long, but it is nicely full and the shimmer is just adorable.
